Customer Segmentation & Financial Analysis

A business intelligence project that analyzes customer demographics, coverage amounts, and premium behavior to support data-driven decision-making in the insurance domain.

🔹 Business Problem

Companies serve customers with diverse financial capacities, life stages, and protection needs. However, policy design, pricing, and targeting decisions are often made without a deep understanding of customer financial behavior and demographic differences. As a result: Customers may be offered coverage that does not match their affordability Premium structures may not reflect customers’ income levels and risk profiles High-value and financially stable customers are not clearly distinguished Financially vulnerable segments may face policies that are difficult to sustain These gaps create a mismatch between customer needs leading to lower adoption rates, higher drop-off rates, and reduced customer lifetime value. From a business perspective, this weak alignment affects revenue growth, profitability, and long-term customer relationships.

🔹 Solution Overview

  • Analyzes customer demographics such as age, gender, occupation, and geography
  • Examines coverage amount and premium distributions across customer segments
  • Identifies high-value and risk-sensitive customer groups
  • Supports strategic decision-making using interactive Power BI dashboards

🔹 Customer Demographic Analysis

Customer Demographic Overview

This analysis focuses on understanding the composition of the customer base using demographic attributes such as age group, gender, occupation, education level, and location. These insights help businesses identify dominant customer groups, regional concentration, and workforce profiles that influence insurance demand.

🔹 Segmentation by Income and Age

Income & Age-Based Customer Segmentation

Customers were segmented based on income levels and age groups to understand how financial capacity influences insurance coverage and premium choices. This segmentation enables insurance businesses to align products with affordability and improve targeting of premium and coverage offerings.

🔹 Business Impact

  • Improved understanding of customer financial behavior
  • Better alignment of coverage and premium strategies
  • Identification of high-value customer segments
  • Enhanced decision-making using visual analytics
  • Support for targeted marketing and policy optimization

🔹 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Power BI–driven analytics can transform raw insurance data into meaningful business insights. By combining customer demographics with financial analysis, insurance companies can make informed decisions related to segmentation, pricing, and policy design.

The dashboard-based approach ensures clarity, accessibility, and scalability for business stakeholders.
